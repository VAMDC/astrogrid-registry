Core metadata: date and version

The date and version refer to the registered resource. If the resource itself has a well-defined version and publication date then enter those. Otherwise, make up something; these fields are mandatory and may not be left blank.

The date should be in a specific format. Enter either just the date in the format YYYY-MM-DD (e.g. 2007-10-17 for 17th October 2007 AD) or the date and time in the format YYYY-MM-DDThh:mm:ss (e.g. 2007-10-17T12:34:56 for 34 minutes and 56 seconds past 12 noon on 17th October 2007 AD). In the latter case, time is always in UTC and no time zone or DST marker may be added.

The version field is free text.
