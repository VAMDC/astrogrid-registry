Status of a registry entry

An entry is in one or three states:

active
The entry is current and the registered resource is available. Put your entry in this state when you are ready for people to read it.
inactive
The registered resource is unavailable, or its registry entry is not yet complete (see below). Put your entry in this state while you are adding information, or if your resource is off-line for some lengthy period (greater than a week, say).
deleted
The registered resource is permanently gone. The registry entry is retained as a record.

All entries in this registry are created in the inactive state. This avoids them being shown to users before you complete the information. You must change the state to active when you want your entry to be visible to users.
